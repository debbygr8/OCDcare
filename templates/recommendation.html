OCD Management & Treatment Recommendations

Mild OCD

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) with Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP).
  • Low-dose SSRIs may be considered (e.g., Fluoxetine) if symptoms interfere with daily life.
  • Light physical activities like walking, yoga, and mindfulness are highly recommended.

Moderate OCD

  • Weekly CBT-ERP sessions with a certified therapist.
  • Medications like Sertraline or Escitalopram under medical supervision.
  • Regular journaling, aerobic exercise, and participation in support groups.

Severe OCD

  • Intensive outpatient or inpatient treatment programs are recommended.
  • Higher doses or combination of SSRIs, monitored by a psychiatrist.
  • Supervised physical activity and complementary therapy such as art or music therapy.
